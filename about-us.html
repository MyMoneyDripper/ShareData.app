Your Trusted Guide to Passive Internet Income

At ShareData.app, we believe that the bandwidth you don't use shouldn't go to waste. Hundreds of thousands of people are turning their idle internet connection into passive income by sharing bandwidth with market research firms and content delivery networks.

Our Mission

We cut through the confusion and misleading claims. ShareData.app is an independent resource dedicated to rigorously testing and reviewing bandwidth sharing apps.

We are not a bandwidth sharing app. We are a consumer advocacy platform.

Transparency & Revenue

To maintain our total independence, ShareData.app does not accept sponsorships or display third-party advertisements. Our only source of revenue is through affiliate links.

Our philosophy is simple: "We make money when you make money." This means we are directly incentivized to only point you toward the safest, most reliable, and highest-paying bandwidth sharing platforms available.

What We Provide

  • Unbiased Rankings: Objective scores based on real-world earnings and payment reliability.
  • Safety First: In-depth reviews that assess the risks of sharing your IP address.
  • Maximum Returns: Tools to help you identify the apps that maximize your Effective Earning Rate (EER).

Our goal is simple: To empower you to turn your unused bandwidth into reliable, passive cash flow.
