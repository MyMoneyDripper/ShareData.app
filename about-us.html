Your Trusted Guide to Passive Internet Income

At ShareData.app, we believe that the bandwidth you don't use shouldn't go to waste. Hundreds of thousands of people are turning their idle internet connection into passive income by sharing bandwidth with market research firms, content delivery networks, and corporations.

But how do you know which apps are legitimate, secure, and offer the best return?

Our Mission

We cut through the confusion and misleading claims. ShareData.app is an independent resource dedicated to rigorously testing and reviewing popular bandwidth sharing apps like Honeygain, PacketStream, and others.

We are not a bandwidth sharing app. We are a consumer advocacy platform.

What We Provide

  • Unbiased Rankings: We provide clear, objective rankings based on critical factors like real-world earnings, payment reliability, and security risk.
  • Safety First: Our in-depth reviews assess the hidden risks of sharing your IP address, helping you choose services that won't compromise your safety or online reputation.
  • Maximum Returns: We help you identify the apps that truly maximize your Effective Earning Rate (EER) based on your location and device setup.

Our goal is simple: To empower you to make informed decisions and safely turn your unused bandwidth into reliable, passive cash flow.
